## 🎯 Choose Your Path to Profit

Pick the business that matches your skills, resources, and interests. All guides are tailored for **Bela Bela, Limpopo, South Africa** with local prices in Rand (R) and consideration for local climate, resources, and market conditions.

### 🧱 [Brick Making](guides/brick-making/)

- **Time to profit:** 2-4 weeks

## 📖 How to Use This Guide

1. **Read all options** - Understand what each business needs
2. **Count your money** - Know how much you can invest
3. **Check your skills** - Choose what you can do or learn
4. **Start small** - Begin with one business, grow slowly
5. **Learn from others** - Find successful people doing this work
6. **Keep records** - Write down income and expenses

## ✅ Success Tips

- **Start with what you know** - Use existing skills first
- **Buy used tools** - Save money on equipment
- **Find free training** - Learn from family, friends, community
- **Test your market** - Make sure people will buy
- **Reinvest profits** - Grow your business slowly
- **Keep learning** - Improve your skills over time
