## 🎯 Best Crops to Start With

### Quick-Growing Vegetables (1-3 months) 🥬

#### Leafy Greens (Fastest Money)
**Examples:** Spinach, lettuce, kale, amaranth

**What You Need:**
- **Land:** Small plot (5m x 5m enough to start)
- **Seeds:** $5-10
- **Tools:** Hoe, watering can, bucket
- **Money to start:** $20-40

**Steps:**
1. **Prepare soil** - Clear weeds, dig/turn soil
2. **Plant seeds** - Follow spacing on seed packet (or ask seed seller)
3. **Water daily** - Morning or evening
4. **Weed weekly** - Keep area clean
5. **Harvest** - 30-45 days, when leaves big enough

**Profit:**
- **Cost:** $20-40 total
- **Harvest:** 20-30 kg per 5m x 5m
- **Sell:** $1-2 per kg
- **Profit:** $20-60 per harvest
- **Can plant:** Every 2 months

---

#### Tomatoes 🍅
**Popular and profitable**

**What You Need:**
- **Land:** 10m x 10m plot
- **Materials:** Seeds/seedlings, stakes or sticks, string
- **Tools:** Hoe, watering can
- **Money to start:** $30-60

**Steps:**
1. **Start seedlings** - Small pots or seed bed (2-3 weeks)
2. **Prepare field** - Dig holes 50cm apart
3. **Plant seedlings** - When 15cm tall
4. **Stake plants** - Tie to stick as they grow
5. **Water regularly** - Don't let soil dry out
6. **Harvest** - 60-90 days, when red/yellow

**Profit:**
- **Cost:** $30-60
- **Harvest:** 100-200 kg
- **Sell:** $0.50-1.50 per kg
- **Profit:** $50-300 per harvest

---

#### Onions 🧅
**Easy to store and sell**

**What You Need:**
- **Land:** 10m x 10m plot
- **Seeds:** Onion sets or seeds
- **Money to start:** $25-50

**Steps:**
1. **Prepare soil** - Well-dug, flat beds
2. **Plant** - Sets 10cm apart, or sow seeds
3. **Water** - Keep moist but not wet
4. **Weed** - Keep clean
5. **Harvest** - When tops fall over (90-120 days)
6. **Dry** - In shade for 2 weeks before selling

**Profit:**
- **Cost:** $25-50
- **Harvest:** 80-150 kg
- **Sell:** $0.70-1.50 per kg
- **Profit:** $56-225 per harvest

### Longer-Term Crops (3-6 months) 🌽

#### Maize/Corn
**Staple crop everyone needs**

**What You Need:**
- **Land:** 20m x 20m minimum
- **Seeds:** $15-30
- **Fertilizer:** Optional but helps
- **Money to start:** $40-80

**Steps:**
1. **Plant at rainy season start**
2. **Space:** 75cm between rows, 25cm in row
3. **Weed** - 2-3 times before harvest
4. **Harvest** - When cobs dry (4-5 months)
5. **Dry well** - Prevent mold

**Profit:**
- **Cost:** $40-80
- **Harvest:** 300-600 kg
- **Sell:** $0.30-0.60 per kg
- **Profit:** $90-360 per harvest

---

#### Beans
**Good protein, nitrogen for soil**

**What You Need:**
- **Land:** 15m x 15m
- **Seeds:** $20-40
- **Money to start:** $30-60

**Steps:**
1. **Plant** - 30cm spacing, 3-4 seeds per hole
2. **Stake** - If climbing variety
3. **Weed** - Keep area clean
4. **Harvest** - Green beans (2 months) or dry beans (3-4 months)

**Profit:**
- **Cost:** $30-60
- **Harvest:** 80-150 kg
- **Sell:** $0.80-1.50 per kg
- **Profit:** $64-225 per harvest

## 🌱 Year-Round Business Plan

### Rainy Season Focus:
- Maize, beans, peppers
- Plant early in season
- Focus on staple crops

### Dry Season (with irrigation):
- Leafy greens, tomatoes, onions
- Higher prices
- Plant near water source

### All Year:
- Keep something growing always
- Rotate crops
- Don't plant same thing in same spot twice

---

## 💧 Water Management

### Without Irrigation:
- **Plant timing** - Start of rains
- **Mulch** - Cover soil with grass/leaves
- **Drought-resistant** - Choose varieties for your area

### With Water Access:
- **Bucket irrigation** - Water morning/evening
- **Drip system** - Save water (can make with bottles)
- **Value crops** - Grow vegetables for higher profit

## 📈 Growing Your Farm Business

### Month 1: Start Small
- Clear and plant 25-50m²
- Choose 1-2 crops you know
- Learn your soil and water needs

### Month 2-4: Expand
- Add 50-100m² more land
- Add second crop type
- Start selling harvest

### Month 6+: Scale Up
- Expand to 200-500m²
- Grow 3-4 different crops
- Hire occasional help
- Consider renting more land

## 🎯 Finding Customers

### Where to Sell:
1. **Local market** - Fresh vegetables daily
2. **Neighbors** - Door to door
3. **Restaurants** - Regular orders
4. **Schools** - Bulk orders
5. **Roadside stand** - Near your farm

### Pricing:
- **Check market prices** - Visit market weekly
- **Fresh = Higher price** - Sell harvest day
- **Bundle deals** - 3 for price of 2
- **Regular customers** - Small discount for loyalty

## ⚠️ Common Problems and Solutions

### Problem: Plants dying
**Solution:**
- Too much or too little water - adjust
- Pests - use local remedies (neem, ash, soap water)
- Disease - remove sick plants, don't overwater

### Problem: Low harvest
**Solution:**
- Poor soil - add compost/manure
- Wrong season - plant at right time
- Too close together - give plants space
- Not enough water - water more regularly

### Problem: Can't sell all harvest
**Solution:**
- Preserve/dry excess
- Give to neighbors (they'll remember)
- Plant less next time
- Find buyers BEFORE harvest

## 💡 Extra Money Saving Tips

✅ **Make compost** - Free fertilizer from kitchen/farm waste
✅ **Save seeds** - From best plants for next season
✅ **Share tools** - Borrow/lend with neighbors
✅ **Start small** - Don't waste money on big plot first
✅ **Learn free** - Ask successful farmers for advice
✅ **Multi-crop** - Grow 3-4 crops, not just one

## 📝 Daily Work Routine

**Early Morning (6am-9am):**
- Water plants
- Harvest ready crops
- Check for problems

**Late Afternoon (4pm-6pm):**
- Weed or plant new areas
- Water if needed
- Prepare for market

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Go to market (sell and check prices)
- Clear weeds thoroughly
- Plan next planting

---

## 🌟 Success Tips

1. **Start NOW** - Don't wait for perfect conditions
2. **One crop well** - Better than many crops poorly
3. **Learn your land** - Every plot different
4. **Talk to farmers** - Ask questions, share knowledge
5. **Keep trying** - First harvest may be small, you'll improve
6. **Record keeping** - Write down what worked and costs

---

**Remember: Even small plot can make money. Start with what you have!**
