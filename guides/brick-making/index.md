## 📊 Quick Comparison - Three Types of Bricks

### 1. Adobe Bricks (Sun-Dried Bricks) 🌞
**Easiest to start - No special equipment needed**
👉 **[Read Complete Adobe Guide](adobe-bricks.md)**

#### What You Need:
- **Materials:** Clay soil, water, straw or grass
- **Tools:** Wooden mold (can make yourself), shovel, bucket
- **Space:** Open area to dry bricks (10m x 10m minimum)
- **Money to start:** R50-100

#### Step by Step:
1. **Find clay soil** - Dig where soil is sticky when wet
2. **Mix ingredients:**
   - 3 parts clay soil
   - 1 part sand (if soil very sticky)
   - Water (make thick like porridge)
   - Handful of chopped straw per bucket
3. **Make mold** - Wooden frame: 30cm x 15cm x 10cm
4. **Pour mixture** - Fill mold, smooth top, let sit 5 minutes
5. **Remove mold** - Lift carefully, leave brick to dry
6. **Dry bricks** - 7-14 days in sun, turn after 3 days
7. **Test brick** - Should be hard, not break easily

#### Selling Price:
- **Cost per brick:** R0.10-0.20
- **Sell for:** R0.30-0.50
- **Profit:** R0.20-0.30 per brick
- **Make 50 bricks per day:** R10-15 profit

---

### 2. Fired Bricks (Kiln Bricks) 🔥
**Stronger bricks - Need more investment**
👉 **[Read Complete Fired Bricks Guide](fired-bricks.md)**

#### What You Need:
- **Materials:** Clay soil, water, fuel (wood, coal, or waste)
- **Tools:** Molds, kiln (can build yourself), wheelbarrow
- **Space:** Large area for kiln and storage
- **Money to start:** R150-300

#### Step by Step:
1. **Make bricks** - Same as adobe (without straw)
2. **Let dry** - 3-5 days until completely dry
3. **Build kiln:**
   - Stack bricks with small gaps
   - Leave channels for fire underneath
   - Cover with mud and broken bricks
4. **Fire bricks:**
   - Start small fire, increase slowly
   - Keep burning 24-48 hours
   - Let cool 24-48 hours
5. **Remove bricks** - Should ring like bell when tapped

#### Selling Price:
- **Cost per brick:** R0.20-0.30
- **Sell for:** R0.50-0.80
- **Profit:** R0.30-0.50 per brick
- **Fire 500 bricks per batch:** R150-250 profit

---

### 3. Compressed Bricks (Machine Bricks) 💪
**Fastest method - Need machine investment**
👉 **[Read Complete Compressed Bricks Guide](compressed-bricks.md)**

#### What You Need:
- **Materials:** Clay soil, cement (small amount), water
- **Tools:** Manual brick press machine
- **Space:** Work area and drying space
- **Money to start:** R200-400 (includes machine)

#### Step by Step:
1. **Prepare mixture:**
   - 10 parts soil
   - 1 part cement
   - Water (make firm, not wet)
2. **Use machine** - Follow machine instructions
3. **Press brick** - Takes 30 seconds per brick
4. **Dry brick** - 7-14 days, spray water daily
5. **Stack carefully** - Don't move for first 3 days

#### Selling Price:
- **Cost per brick:** R0.15-0.25
- **Sell for:** R0.40-0.70
- **Profit:** R0.25-0.45 per brick
- **Make 200 bricks per day:** R50-90 profit

## 🎯 Finding Customers

### Where to Sell:
1. **Construction sites** - Visit builders, offer samples
2. **Hardware stores** - Ask to supply them
3. **Community** - Tell neighbors, church, mosque
4. **Signs** - Put sign where you work
5. **Word of mouth** - Good quality = customers tell others

### Pricing Strategy:
- **Start lower** - Build reputation first
- **Bulk discount** - Lower price for big orders
- **Deliver free** - For large orders nearby
- **Quality first** - Better to sell less at good quality

## ⚠️ Common Problems and Solutions

### Problem: Bricks crack when drying
**Solution:** 
- Mix has too much water - use less next time
- Drying too fast - cover with cloth/leaves
- Soil wrong type - add more sand

### Problem: Bricks too weak
**Solution:**
- Not enough clay in soil - find better soil
- Dried too quickly - dry slower
- Adobe needs more straw
- Fired bricks not fired long enough

### Problem: Not enough customers
**Solution:**
- Show good quality bricks to builders
- Offer small test order free
- Visit construction sites early morning
- Make sure your price is competitive

✅ **Learn from others** - Visit successful brick makers
