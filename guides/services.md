## 🎯 Service Business Options

### 1. Cleaning Services 🧹
**Always needed, minimal investment**

**What You Can Offer:**

**House Cleaning:**
- Regular weekly/monthly cleaning
- Deep cleaning (spring cleaning)
- Moving in/out cleaning
- After-party cleanup

**Office/Shop Cleaning:**
- Daily or weekly service
- Floor cleaning and mopping
- Window cleaning
- Bathroom cleaning

**What You Need:**
- **Supplies:** Brooms, mops, buckets, cloths, soap
- **Money to start:** $20-40

**Pricing:**
- **Small house:** $5-10 per clean
- **Large house:** $10-20 per clean
- **Office:** $15-30 per month
- **Deep clean:** $20-40
- **Hourly rate:** $2-4

**How to Start:**
1. Start with family/friends
2. Ask for referrals
3. Do excellent work
4. Build regular clients
5. Offer package deals (weekly/monthly)

**Daily Income:**
- 2-3 houses per day
- **Profit:** $10-30 per day

3. Set regular days for customers

## 📈 Starting Your Service Business

### Week 1: Choose Service
- **Consider:** Your skills, local needs, competition
- **Start:** With what you know/can learn quickly
- **Test:** Offer to few people first

### Week 2-4: Build Base
- **Offer:** Low introductory prices
- **Quality:** Do excellent work
- **Ask:** For referrals from satisfied customers
- **Schedule:** Set regular times/days

### Month 2+: Grow
- **Expand:** Take more clients
- **Raise:** Prices to fair level
- **Improve:** Skills and service quality
- **Specialize:** Focus on most profitable

## 🎯 Finding Customers

### Marketing Your Services:

**Word of Mouth:**
- Tell everyone what you do
- Ask satisfied customers to tell others
- Offer small incentive for referrals

**Community:**
- Announce at church/mosque
- Post on community boards
- Tell at market/shops
- Network at gatherings

**Direct Approach:**
- Knock on doors in your area
- Introduce yourself politely
- Offer first service discount
- Leave contact information

**Build Reputation:**
✅ Always on time
✅ Do what you promise
✅ Go extra mile
✅ Be honest and trustworthy
✅ Professional appearance

---

## 💰 Pricing Your Services

### How to Set Prices:

**Research:**
1. Ask what others charge
2. Check local rates
3. Consider your expenses
4. Value your time

**Formula:**
- **Time** x **Fair hourly rate** + **Materials** + **Transport** = **Price**

**Adjustments:**
- **Regular customers:** Small discount
- **One-time:** Full price
- **Multiple services:** Package deal
- **Urgent/weekend:** Premium rate

## ⚠️ Common Problems and Solutions

### Problem: Customers not paying
**Solution:**
- Get payment upfront for materials
- Payment immediately after service
- Written agreement for regular clients
- Don't extend credit unless trusted customer

### Problem: Not enough customers
**Solution:**
- Lower prices temporarily
- Offer first-time discount
- Improve marketing
- Ask customers to refer others
- Expand service area

### Problem: Too much work alone
**Solution:**
- Hire helper for big jobs
- Partner with others
- Focus on most profitable services
- Raise prices (quality over quantity)

### Problem: Customers not satisfied
**Solution:**
- Ask what's wrong
- Fix it immediately
- Learn from feedback
- Set clear expectations upfront
