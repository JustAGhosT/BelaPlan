# 🏪 Shop Keeping Guide

## Make Money Running a Small Shop

**Everyone needs to buy things daily!**

---

## 🎯 Types of Shops You Can Start

### 1. Home Shop/Kiosk (Easiest Start) 🏠
**Sell from your home or small structure**

**What to Sell:**
- **Daily needs:** Sugar, salt, tea, oil, flour
- **Snacks:** Biscuits, candy, drinks
- **Household:** Soap, matches, candles
- **Personal:** Airtime, batteries

**What You Need:**
- **Space:** Room in home, small window, or kiosk
- **Stock:** Start small - most needed items
- **Money to start:** R50-150

**How to Start:**
1. **Survey neighbors** - What do they need?
2. **Start small** - 20-30 basic items
3. **Buy wholesale** - At market early morning
4. **Price fairly** - Check other shop prices
5. **Record everything** - What sells, what doesn't

**Daily Profit:**
- Buy: R50 stock
- Mark up: 20-30%
- Sell: R60-65
- **Profit:** R10-15 per day (starting)
- **Reinvest** to grow stock

## 📈 Starting Your Shop Business

### Week 1: Planning
- **Survey area** - What's needed?
- **Check competition** - What do they sell? What prices?
- **Count money** - Know your budget
- **Find suppliers** - Wholesale market or distributors
- **Prepare space** - Clean, organize, secure

### Week 2-4: Opening
- **Buy initial stock** - Start with essentials
- **Price items** - Competitive but profitable
- **Open daily** - Consistent hours
- **Be friendly** - Customer service matters
- **Track sales** - Write everything down

### Month 2-3: Growing
- **Expand stock** - Add what sells well
- **Drop slow items** - Don't tie up money
- **Build credit** - Regular customers can pay weekly
- **Improve display** - Make shop attractive
- **Adjust prices** - Stay competitive

### Month 4+: Established
- **Known in community**
- **Regular customers**
- **Steady income**
- **Consider expansion** - Bigger shop or second location

## 💰 Managing Money in Shop Business

### Buying Stock:

**Where to Buy:**
1. **Wholesale market** - Cheapest prices
2. **Distributors** - Convenient, may deliver
3. **Cash and carry** - Bulk purchases
4. **Manufacturers** - Direct for large quantities

**Tips:**
✅ Buy early morning (best selection)
✅ Buy in bulk (better prices)
✅ Compare prices (different suppliers)
✅ Check quality (expired, damaged)
✅ Keep receipts (return if problem)

### Pricing:

**Simple Formula:**
- **Cost price** + **Markup** = **Selling price**
- Typical markup: 20-40%

**Example:**
- Sugar (1kg): Buy R0.80, Sell R1.00 = R0.20 profit (25%)
- Soap: Buy R0.60, Sell R0.80 = R0.20 profit (33%)

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Common items: Lower markup (compete)
- Special items: Higher markup
- Bulk sales: Small discount
- Stay competitive: Check other shops

## 📊 Record Keeping (Very Important!)

### Daily Records:
- **Sales** - Everything sold
- **Expenses** - Stock bought, bills
- **Cash in hand** - Count at end of day

### Weekly Records:
- **Fast sellers** - Buy more
- **Slow sellers** - Buy less or stop
- **Total profit** - Sales minus costs
- **Stock level** - What to reorder

### Monthly Records:
- **Total income**
- **Total expenses**
- **Profit**
- **Growth** - Compare to last month

**Simple Format:**
```
Date: [date]
Opening money: $___
Sales today: $___
Stock bought: $___
Other expenses: $___
Closing money: $___
Profit today: $___
```

## 🎯 Customer Service

### Be a Good Shop Keeper:
✅ **Smile and greet** - Welcome customers
✅ **Be patient** - Even with small purchases
✅ **Be honest** - Don't cheat on price/weight
✅ **Keep clean** - Shop and yourself
✅ **Be available** - Regular opening hours
✅ **Give correct change** - Count carefully
✅ **Remember names** - Personal touch

### Handle Difficult Situations:
- **Customer wants credit** - Only if you know them well
- **Customer disputes price** - Show marked price calmly
- **Product complaint** - Exchange if genuine problem
- **Shoplifting** - Watch without accusing everyone

### Build Loyalty:
- Remember what customers buy regularly
- Small extra for good customers
- Fair prices always
- Quality products
- Friendly service

---

## 🔒 Security

### Protect Your Shop:
- **Strong locks** - On doors and windows
- **Burglar bars** - If possible
- **Hide money** - Don't keep all in register
- **Bank regularly** - Don't keep too much cash
- **Insure** - If possible
- **Lights** - Security lights at night

### Protect Your Stock:
- **Stock rotation** - Sell old first (FIFO)
- **Check expiry dates** - Don't sell expired
- **Store properly** - Dry, clean, organized
- **Count regularly** - Know what's there
- **Watch for theft** - Aware but not paranoid

## ⚠️ Common Problems and Solutions

### Problem: Running out of stock
**Solution:**
- Keep minimum stock levels
- Restock before running out
- Know supplier delivery times
- Keep emergency cash for urgent restocking

### Problem: Cash flow problems
**Solution:**
- Don't give too much credit
- Don't buy too much slow-selling stock
- Separate business and personal money
- Keep emergency fund

### Problem: Competition
**Solution:**
- Better customer service
- Longer opening hours
- Special items they don't have
- Build personal relationships
- Fair prices (not necessarily cheapest)

### Problem: Slow sales
**Solution:**
- Check if location good
- Review prices (too high?)
- Improve shop appearance
- Add popular items
- Better customer service
- Advertise (word of mouth, signs)

## 📊 Shop Financial Planning

### Starting Capital Breakdown (R200):
- Stock: R150 (75%)
- Shelving/containers: R30 (15%)
- Emergency fund: R20 (10%)

### Growth Strategy:
- **Month 1:** Reinvest 70% profit
- **Month 2-3:** Reinvest 50% profit
- **Month 4+:** Reinvest 30%, save/use 70%

### When to Expand:
- Stock consistently selling out
- Turning over inventory weekly
- Steady daily profit
- Saved enough for bigger stock
- Customer demand for more items
