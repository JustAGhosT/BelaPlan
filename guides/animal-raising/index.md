# 🐓 Animal Raising Guide

## Make Money From Livestock in Bela Bela

**Animals give meat, eggs, milk, and more animals to sell!**

**Location Context:** This guide is tailored for **Bela Bela, Limpopo, South Africa** - considering local climate, available grazing, water access, and market demand from both residents and tourists.

---

## 🎯 Best Animals to Start With

**[→ Complete Poultry Guide](poultry-guide.md)** - Detailed guide for layers and broilers in Bela Bela

### 1. Chickens (Easiest Start) 🐔

#### Layers (Egg Production)
**Daily income from eggs**

**What You Need:**
- **Animals:** 5-10 hens, 1 rooster (optional)
- **Housing:** Simple coop (2m x 2m for 10 chickens)
- **Food:** Kitchen scraps + grain
- **Money to start:** R30-60

**Steps to Start:**
1. **Build/buy coop** - Keep safe from predators, rain
2. **Buy young hens** - 3-4 months old (ready to lay)
3. **Feed twice daily** - Morning and evening
4. **Collect eggs** - Daily
5. **Clean coop** - Weekly

**Profit:**
- **Cost per chicken:** R5-8
- **Feed:** R2-3 per chicken per month
- **Eggs:** 5-6 per week per hen
- **Sell eggs:** R0.20-0.40 each
- **Profit:** R20-40 per month (10 hens)

---

#### Broilers (Meat Production)
**Fast money in 6-8 weeks**

**What You Need:**
- **Animals:** 20-50 chicks
- **Housing:** Larger coop with heat (first 3 weeks)
- **Food:** Special broiler feed
- **Money to start:** R60-150

**Steps:**
1. **Buy day-old chicks**
2. **Keep warm** - Heat lamp/container first 3 weeks
3. **Feed high-protein food** - Follow feed instructions
4. **Sell at 2kg** - Usually 6-8 weeks

**Profit:**
- **Chick:** R1-2
- **Feed per bird:** R3-5
- **Sell at 2kg:** R8-12
- **Profit:** R3-5 per bird
- **20 birds:** R60-100 profit per batch

---

### 2. Goats 🐐
**Good for milk, meat, and breeding**

**What You Need:**
- **Animals:** 2-4 female goats
- **Housing:** Simple shelter, fenced area
- **Food:** Grass, leaves, small amount grain
- **Money to start:** R80-200

**Steps to Start:**
1. **Buy young females** - 4-6 months old
2. **Make shelter** - Keep dry, safe from dogs
3. **Graze or cut grass** - Daily feeding
4. **Breed** - Use neighbor's male or buy one
5. **Sell kids** - Every 6-8 months

**Profit:**
- **Cost per goat:** R40-80
- **Feed:** Mostly free (grass) + R5/month grain
- **Kids born:** 1-2 every 8 months
- **Sell kid:** R30-60 each
- **Milk:** Can sell or use for family
- **Profit:** R60-120 per goat per year

---

### 3. Rabbits 🐰
**Fast breeding, small space needed**

**What You Need:**
- **Animals:** 1 male, 2-3 females
- **Housing:** Hutches/cages (can build from wire/wood)
- **Food:** Grass, leaves, vegetable scraps
- **Money to start:** R40-80

**Steps to Start:**
1. **Build/buy hutches** - Raised off ground
2. **Buy breeding rabbits** - 4-5 months old
3. **Feed daily** - Greens and scraps
4. **Breed** - Put female with male for few hours
5. **Sell young rabbits** - 8-12 weeks old

**Profit:**
- **Cost per rabbit:** R10-20
- **Feed:** Mostly free (grass/scraps)
- **Babies:** 6-8 every 2 months per female
- **Sell at 2kg:** R10-15
- **Profit:** R80-120 per month (3 females)

---

### 4. Pigs 🐷
**Bigger investment, bigger profit**

**What You Need:**
- **Animals:** 1-2 piglets
- **Housing:** Strong pen with shelter
- **Food:** Kitchen waste, grain, water
- **Money to start:** R100-200

**Steps to Start:**
1. **Build strong pen** - Pigs are strong, need secure fence
2. **Buy piglets** - 8-12 weeks old
3. **Feed 2-3 times daily** - Mixed diet
4. **Keep clean** - Healthy pigs grow faster
5. **Sell at 80-100kg** - 5-6 months

**Profit:**
- **Piglet:** R30-50
- **Feed:** R60-100 total
- **Sell at 100kg:** R150-250
- **Profit:** R60-100 per pig
- **Two pigs:** R120-200 profit per 6 months

## 🏥 Keeping Animals Healthy in Bela Bela

### Daily Care:
- **Fresh water** - Always available (critical in hot Bela Bela summers)
- **Enough food** - Morning and evening
- **Clean housing** - Remove waste regularly
- **Check health** - Look for sick animals
- **Heat management** - Provide shade (30-40°C summer temperatures)

### Warning Signs (Sick Animal):
- Not eating
- Not moving much
- Diarrhea
- Coughing
- Lying down all time
- Heat stress (panting excessively in summer)

**Action:** Separate from others, contact local vet or agricultural extension officer

### Prevention:
✅ **Vaccinate** - Contact Modimolle-Mookgophong Municipality agricultural office
✅ **Clean housing** - Prevent disease (more important in hot climate)
✅ **Good food** - Healthy animals resist disease
✅ **Quarantine new animals** - 2 weeks separate before mixing
✅ **Shade structures** - Essential for Bela Bela heat
✅ **Water supply** - Plan for dry winter months

### Local Veterinary Support:
- Bela Bela Veterinary Clinic
- Agricultural extension officers (Modimolle-Mookgophong Municipality)
- State Veterinary Office (Polokwane)
- Farmers' cooperatives in the area

## 🎯 Finding Customers

### Where to Sell:

**Eggs/Milk:**
- Neighbors and lodges
- Local shops and spaza shops
- Guesthouses and B&Bs (Bela Bela has many)
- Hot Springs Resort area
- Regular customers (deliver weekly)
- Bela Bela Saturday market

**Meat/Live Animals:**
- Butchers in Bela Bela
- Saturday market day
- Direct to families (holidays/celebrations)
- Other farmers (breeding animals)
- Restaurants (especially game lodges in area)
- Modimolle market (nearby town)

### Bela Bela Market Advantages:
- **Tourist demand** - Hotels and lodges need consistent supply
- **Growing population** - New residential areas developing
- **Holiday destination** - Peak demand during school holidays
- **Farmland surroundings** - Easy to source feed and grazing

### Pricing:
- **Check market** - Know current prices
- **Quality matters** - Healthy animals worth more
- **Direct selling** - Better price than middleman
- **Build reputation** - Quality = regular customers

## ⚠️ Common Problems and Solutions

### Problem: Animals dying
**Solution:**
- Vaccinate at right age
- Keep housing clean and dry
- Provide good food and clean water
- Isolate sick animals fast

### Problem: Not enough food
**Solution:**
- Grow food crops for animals
- Make partnerships (collect waste from restaurants)
- Buy feed in bulk with other farmers
- Choose animals that eat local food (goats, rabbits)

### Problem: Not selling fast enough
**Solution:**
- Find buyers BEFORE animals ready
- Network with butchers/shops
- Advertise at church/mosque/community center
- Time production for holidays (higher prices)

## 💡 Extra Tips

✅ **Start with hardy breeds** - Local animals survive better
✅ **Keep males separate** - Prevent unwanted breeding
✅ **Record keeping** - Write down births, deaths, sales, costs
✅ **Learn from others** - Visit successful animal raisers
✅ **Join groups** - Farmer groups share knowledge and resources
✅ **Use manure** - Sell or use for crops (extra income)

## 🌟 Money Saving Strategies

1. **Feed from waste** - Kitchen scraps, restaurant waste
2. **Grow animal feed** - Plant grass, vegetables
3. **Build own housing** - Use local materials
4. **Buy young animals** - Cheaper than mature ones
5. **Group buying** - Buy feed/supplies with other farmers
6. **Free veterinary days** - Check with agricultural office

## 📝 Daily Work Routine

**Morning (6am-8am):**
- Feed animals
- Collect eggs
- Check for sick animals
- Clean water containers

**Afternoon (4pm-6pm):**
- Feed again
- Collect eggs
- Clean housing if needed
- Prepare next day's food

**Weekly:**
- Deep clean all housing
- Check animals carefully for problems
- Weigh growing animals
- Calculate feed needs for next week
